    About Us

    Umuegwu Mixed Farm Limited is a trusted Nigerian agribusiness committed to producing healthy, high-quality livestock and farm products for homes, businesses, and markets across the country. We specialize in mixed farming, combining livestock rearing and crop production using modern farming techniques and sustainable practices. Our farm is built on integrity, quality, and consistency. We rear pigs, poultry, goats, and other farm animals under hygienic conditions and provide fresh, affordable, and nutritious products to our customers. We also engage in crop farming, supplying fresh fruits and vegetables directly from our farm to the market. At Umuegwu Mixed Farm Limited, we believe agriculture is the backbone of the nation. That is why we focus on improving food security, creating jobs, and supporting local communities through responsible farming. We are driven by a passion for excellence and a commitment to feeding Nigeria with pride.
